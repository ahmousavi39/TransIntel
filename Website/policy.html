Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 8, 2026

1. Information We Collect

TransIntel ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal information:

  • Translation Text: Text you input for translation is sent to our servers temporarily for processing and is not stored permanently
  • Uploaded Files: Files you upload for translation are processed temporarily and deleted immediately after extraction
  • Language Preferences: Your preferred source and target languages are stored locally on your device
  • Theme Preference: Your light/dark mode preference is stored locally on your device

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide translation services using Google Gemini AI
  • Extract text from uploaded files (images, PDFs, audio)
  • Save your app preferences locally for a better user experience

We do not:

  • Store your translations or uploaded files on our servers
  • Sell, trade, or share your data with third parties for marketing
  • Track your usage beyond basic analytics

3. Third-Party Services

TransIntel uses the following third-party service:

  • Google Gemini AI: For translation and text extraction. Your data is processed according to Google's Privacy Policy

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Uploaded files are processed in memory and deleted immediately
  • No permanent storage of user translations or files
  • Local storage uses secure AsyncStorage

5. Data Retention

TransIntel follows a minimal data retention policy:

  • Translation requests are not logged or stored
  • Uploaded files are deleted immediately after processing
  • Local preferences remain on your device until you delete the app

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear all locally stored preferences through the app settings
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the application
  • Use the app without creating an account

7. Children's Privacy

TransIntel does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
